Questions, answered

Is Aloud free?

Yes — completely free, forever. No sign-up and no email required. If you find it useful, you can leave an optional tip to help keep it running.

How do you keep it running if it’s free?

Aloud is a passion project kept free for everyone. There’s no paywall and no obligation — people who find it handy can leave a small voluntary tip towards the running costs, and that’s it.

Are my PDFs uploaded anywhere?

No. Aloud reads your PDF entirely inside your own browser. The file is never sent to a server, so your documents stay private.

Do I need an account or to install anything?

Neither. Aloud runs in any modern web browser with nothing to install and no account to create.

Can it read scanned PDFs?

Not yet. Aloud reads PDFs that contain real text. A scanned PDF is an image of a page, which would need OCR — that’s on the roadmap.

Will there be more natural-sounding voices?

For now Aloud uses your browser’s built-in voices, which are free and private. More natural-sounding voices may be added in future — and Aloud itself will stay free.
